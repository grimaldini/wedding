OUR STORY

We met through friends, the old school way, in San Francisco in September of 2014. We didn't speak much on our first encounter, but Luis kept coincidentally on purpose being at the same events as Marianne the rest of that weekend. Luis found out Marianne & her friends were going to Santa Cruz to learn how to surf, and offered to come down with his surf board and teach them.

Luis is not a morning person, but at the crack of dawn that Sunday, he was there. Marianne thought he was fun, but the moment she became curious to actually get to know Luis was later at the restaurant when they were playing italian opera, and Luis mentioned "I love the Three Tenors." Marianne was instantly impressed, since not many people enjoy opera these days, and Marianne loves it!

THE PROPOSAL

For their 5 year dating anniversary, Luis took Marianne on a trip to the Middle East in Nov of 2019. They saw enchanting sights in Istanbul, Egypt, and Jordan. Luis made a sketch of all the key landmarks along the way.

A few days before the end of the trip, they visited the landmark Marianne was looking forward to the most: Petra! Luis and Marianne woke up before the sunrise and did the hike up to see The Monestary, one of the legendary monuments carved into the rock. Luis suggested to go up to the best viewpoint and take a picture. Luis then handed Marianne the journal he had been sketching in during the trip, and asked her to flip to the last page. Marianne opened it, and what she saw was a sketch of the Monestary, from the exact point where they were standing now. And in the sketch, Luis was down on one knee with a ring. Surprised, she looked up, and Luis was down on one knee just like the sketch, and asked her to marry him. The most amazing and biggest surprise in the world - and of course Marianne said yes! Or actually, "Yay!" because she was lost for words.
